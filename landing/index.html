Volunteer Now
The Problem

Volunteering is stuck in 2005.

You want to help. You really do. But finding opportunities means wading through stale listings on outdated websites, filling out long applications, and hoping someone emails you back in a week.

Meanwhile, nonprofits are desperate for volunteers but can't compete for attention in a world of 15-second content. Their mission gets buried under walls of text that nobody reads.

The gap between "I want to help" and "I'm signed up" is way too wide.

The Solution

What if finding a cause was as easy as scrolling your feed?

SwipeForCause gives nonprofits the power of short-form video to showcase their mission ‚Äî and gives you a fast, beautiful way to discover causes that actually resonate.

No more job-board listings. No more 10-step applications. Just scroll, watch, feel something, and tap "Volunteer Now." That's it. The nonprofit gets your info and reaches out.

Real organizations. Real impact. One tap away.

Dead simple, by design.

Whether you want to give time or find help, it takes less than a minute.
